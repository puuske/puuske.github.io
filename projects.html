Chained 2 Violence

About the game:
Chained 2 Violence is a chaotic, fast-paced action game where players battle through hordes of enemies while physically chained to their co-op partner. Timing, coordination, and a healthy dose of mayhem is required to survive.

My Role:
I was the lead systems designer and gameplay programmer. I developed the enemy AI behaviors, implemented the chaining mechanic, and helped shape the game loop. I also contributed to playtesting and balancing.
